Secure Synthetic Energy Data with Generative AI

Dai Dong, Sam West, Mahathir Almashor

Introduction

Many countries, including Australia, have committed to achieving net zero by 2050, and data sharing in the energy sector is crucial to achieving this goal. However, data sharing is often hindered by concerns about privacy and legal issues. Data synthesis presents a promising solution to these challenges. Our project aims to generate time-series energy data using a Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) model. Additionally, we implement privacy protection filters within the generative model to safeguard against potential attacks, ensuring the security and privacy of the synthesized data.
